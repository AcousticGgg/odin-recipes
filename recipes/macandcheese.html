Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a comforting, creamy dish made with tender elbow macaroni coated in a rich cheese sauce. It’s smooth, savory, and satisfying, often served as a main dish or a hearty side.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
    • Optional additions:
  • Paprika or mustard powder
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta

    2. Boil the macaroni in salted water according to package directions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the roux

    3. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Stir in the flour and cook for about 1 minute, until smooth and lightly bubbly.

  3. Add the milk

    4. Slowly whisk in the milk, stirring constantly to prevent lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens slightly.

  4. Add the cheese

    5. Lower the heat and stir in the shredded cheddar until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper.

  5. Combine

    6. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir until fully coated.

  6. Serve (or bake, optional)

    7. Serve immediately, or transfer to a baking dish, top with breadcrumbs, and bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 15 minutes until golden.
