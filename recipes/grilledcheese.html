Grilled Cheese

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a simple, comforting classic made with crispy, golden-brown bread and warm, gooey melted cheese in the center.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1-2 slices of cheese (cheddar, american)
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place one slice, butter-side down, in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the cheese on top, then place the second slice of bread on top, butter-side up.
  4. Cook until the bottom is golden brown, then flip and cook the other side until golden and the cheese is melted.
  5. Remove from the pan, slice if desired, and enjoy while warm.
